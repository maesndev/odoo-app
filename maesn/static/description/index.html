What is maesn?

maesn is a Unified API platform built in Germany that lets SaaS companies, fintechs and banks launch integrations with Odoo and more than 50 other European business tools in days instead of months. Instead of building and maintaining every ERP, accounting or HR integration yourself, you integrate once with maesn and get access to an entire category of systems through a single, normalized data model: including DATEV, SAP, Microsoft Dynamics, Sage, Visma, Exact Online and, of course, Odoo.

Companies like Tipalti (US payables automation), Rally (Y Combinator), Findity (spend management) and Agicap (cash flow management) use maesn to offer their customers native accounting and ERP integrations without building them in-house.

What does this module do?

This module adds a maesn menu entry to your Odoo instance that takes you directly to the maesn Odoo integration page, where you can explore all available integrations and connect your Odoo data to the rest of your tool stack.

Note: maesn is an external service. Using the maesn platform requires a maesn account. The module itself is free and does not modify or transfer any of your Odoo data.

What can you sync between Odoo and other tools?

Accounting & ERP

Sync invoices, journal entries, contacts, payments and tax data between Odoo and accounting systems like DATEV, Sage, Visma, Exact Online or SAP. Ideal for spend management, invoicing, lending and cash flow platforms.

HR & ATS

Access employee records, absences and applicant data through one normalized model, so your HR tech product works with Odoo and every other HRIS your customers run.

Project Management

Read and write projects, tasks and time entries across Odoo and other PM tools, without maintaining separate integrations for each system.

How it works

  1. Install this module from the Odoo Apps Store (free).
  2. Open the maesn menu in your Odoo instance and create your account on maesn.com/integrations/odoo.
  3. Connect Odoo and any of the 50+ supported tools. maesn handles authentication, data normalization and syncing through one Unified API.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect Odoo to DATEV, SAP or Sage?

With maesn you do not build point-to-point connections. You connect your product once to the maesn Unified API and instantly support Odoo, DATEV, SAP, Sage, Visma, Exact Online and more. Details and the full connector list are available on the maesn Odoo integration page.

Is this module free?

Yes. The module is free and open. The maesn platform itself is a commercial service with plans based on the number of connections and API usage.

Who is maesn for?

maesn is built for SaaS companies, fintechs, neobanks and banks that want to offer their customers integrations with Odoo and other ERP, accounting, HR and project management systems, without building and maintaining each one internally. Odoo implementation partners also use maesn to connect client systems faster.

Is maesn GDPR compliant?

Yes. maesn is developed and operated by maesn GmbH, based in Düsseldorf, Germany, and is fully GDPR compliant. Data processing agreements and EU hosting are part of the standard setup, and the platform supports regulatory requirements such as DORA for financial services customers.

Does the module change my Odoo data?

No. This module only adds a menu entry. Any data synchronization is configured explicitly by you on the maesn platform, with full control over scopes and connected systems.

Which Odoo versions are supported?

The maesn Odoo connector supports current Odoo versions, both Community and Enterprise, including Odoo Online and Odoo.sh. See the developer documentation for the full compatibility matrix.
